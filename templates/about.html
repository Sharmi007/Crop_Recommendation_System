Who We Are

The Crop Recommendation System is designed to assist farmers in making informed decisions about which crops to plant, based on their soil parameters and weather data. By leveraging Machine Learning and IoT-based weather APIs, we provide insights that lead to better yields and sustainable farming.

Our goal is to help modernize agriculture through smart technology while keeping simplicity and accessibility at the core for every farmer.
